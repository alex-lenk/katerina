I am a Russian citizen born in Astrakhan, Russia and raised by a university teacher and a business owner. My upbringing gave me a harmonious influence of education, culture, business, and true prosperity in many ways. I have taken the best of both worlds: Europe’s sophistication and cultural depth and American upbeat attitude and passion for life and combined them to create the versatile being I am today!

I've been featured in numerous TV shows and regularly film video blogs devoted to my healthy lifestyle and exciting daily adventures. I have a masters degree in linguistics and interpreting and I speak fluent Russian, German, and English.

I put my heart into everything I do whether it’s tough gym workout or creating my new fashion collection. I design all my competition costumes, swimwear pieces and unique wardrobes for my glamour photo shoots.

My site allows me to show YOU my multiple ways of self-expression, follow my fitness training and competition prep, pack you in my luggage bag during my travels, introduce you to the hottest fitness models from all the corners of the globe, but most of all to show you what my amazing life is all about!!
